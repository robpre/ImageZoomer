# Privacy Policy

ImageZoomer does not collect, store, sell, transmit, or share personal data.

The extension runs locally in the user's browser. It reads image-related elements on webpages only to provide its zoom, pan, rotate, and hover-preview features.

ImageZoomer uses Chrome storage only to remember local extension settings, such as whether auto-hover preview is enabled and the preferred preview window size. These settings are not sent to any server.

ImageZoomer does not track browsing history, does not use analytics, does not include ads, and does not use remote code.
